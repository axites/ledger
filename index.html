Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our AML checks of crypto wallets.

What is Ledger Scanner?

Ledger Scanner is a service for checking crypto wallets for compliance with international anti-money laundering standards. We help ensure that your transactions comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

What data is required to verify a wallet?

To start working with our service and check your wallet, you need to register. During the registration process, you will need to specify your email, link your wallet and verify it. After registration, you will be able to add your crypto wallet addresses for AML checks.

How long does the verification process take?

The check usually takes no more than a few minutes. However, depending on the current system load and the complexity of the analysis, this process can take up to several hours.

My wallet failed verification?

If your wallet has not passed the verification, we recommend that you consult with our support service to clarify possible reasons and receive recommendations on further actions.

Checking the organization's wallets

Our service is suitable for both individual users and organizations. We offer special corporate plans and tools for monitoring and managing multiple wallets.

What security measures does Ledger Scanner use?

We maintain strict security standards, including data encryption and the use of secure servers. All transaction and verification data is stored in encrypted form to ensure the privacy and security of user information.
